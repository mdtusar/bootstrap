Product description UVEX The German sports label Uvex is regarded as the classic among sports brands. Since its establishment in 1926, the family business has set itself the goal of uncompromising protection in sport, leisure and work. The brand name, which can be traced back to optimum eye protection, also results from this claim: Uv Exclusion stands for protection against ultraviolet radiation, which all sports models offer you thanks to the integrated 100% UV filter.
